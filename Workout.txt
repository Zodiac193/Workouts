Back:

1. Warm-up:
 jumping jacks 
 bodyweight squats 
 arm circles 

2. Pull-ups (4 sets x 8-12 reps): 4×10

3. Bent-Over Rows (4 sets x 10-12 reps):
Grab your two 5kg dumbbells and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees slightly and hinge forward at the hips keeping your back flat. Let your arms hang naturally with a dumbbell in each hand palms facing each other. Engage your core and pull the dumbbells up toward your chest squeezing your shoulder blades together. Lower the dumbbells back down with control and repeat for the desired number of reps.

4. Renegade Rows (3 sets x 10-12 reps):
Place the dumbbells on the floor shoulder-width apart. Start in a push-up position with your hands gripping the dumbbells. Keeping your core engaged and your hips stable lift one dumbbell off the ground and pull it up towards your chest in a rowing motion. Lower it back down and repeat with the other arm. Alternate sides with controlled movements focusing on keeping your body stable throughout the exercise.

5. Reverse Flyes (3 sets x 12-15 reps):
Hold a dumbbell in each hand with a neutral grip (palms facing each other). Bend forward at the hips maintaining a slight bend in your knees and keep your back flat. Extend your arms straight down in front of you then raise them out to the sides squeezing your shoulder blades together as you lift. Lower the dumbbells back down and repeat for the desired number of reps.

Shoulders:
	Warm-Up (5 minutes):
	
	1	Arm circles: 1 minute
	2	Neck stretches: 1 minute
	3	Shoulder stretches: 1 minute
	4	Push-ups: 2 minutes (to engage the shoulder muscles)
	--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Main Workout (20 minutes):
		Perform each exercise for 45 seconds, followed by a 15-second rest. Complete 3 Rounds.
	
	1- Dumbbell Shoulder Press (using household items as weights):	
		Sit or stand with a weight in each hand at shoulder height.
		Press the weights overhead, extending your arms fully.
		Lower the weights back to shoulder height.

	2- Lateral Raises (using household items as weights):	
		Stand with a weight in each hand by your sides.
		Lift your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground.
		Lower the weights back to your sides.

	3- Front Raises (using household items as weights):	
		Stand with a weight in each hand in front of your thighs.
		Lift your arms in front of you until they are parallel to the ground.
		Lower the weights back to your thighs.

	4- Reverse Flies (using household items as weights):	
		Bend at the hips to a 45-degree angle while holding a weight in each hand.
		Lift your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground.
		Lower the weights back to the starting position.

	5- Push-Ups (Wide Grip):	
		Get into a push-up position with your hands placed slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
		Lower your chest towards the ground, then push back up to the starting position.
	--------------------------------------------------------------------------
	Cool Down (5 minutes):	
		1- Neck and shoulder stretch: 2 minutes
		2- Arm stretches: 2 minutes
		4- Standing forward bend: 1 minute
